Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 25, 2026

RevivaCatch LLC ("RevivaCatch," "we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit revivacatch.com, contact us, or use our services.

Information we collect

  • Contact details you provide: name, email address, phone number, business name.
  • Business information shared during an audit, demo, or onboarding.
  • Website usage data: pages visited, device/browser info, and cookies.

How we use your information

  • To provide and operate our missed-call recovery, booking, and review services.
  • To respond to inquiries and schedule consultations.
  • To send service-related and, with your consent, marketing communications by email and SMS.
  • To improve our services and website.

SMS / text messaging

By providing your phone number and opting in, you consent to receive SMS text messages from RevivaCatch related to appointment scheduling, missed-call follow-up, account and service updates, and — where you have agreed — promotions.

  • Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply.
  • Reply STOP at any time to opt out. Reply HELP for help.
  • Opting out of SMS will not affect your ability to use our other services.

No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Information sharing with subcontractors strictly for support services (such as customer service or message delivery) is permitted. All other categories exclude text-messaging originator opt-in data and consent — this information will not be shared with any third parties.

How we share information

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only with trusted service providers who help us operate (such as our platform and messaging providers), only as needed to deliver our services, or where required by law.

Data security & retention

We use reasonable safeguards to protect your information and retain it only as long as needed to provide services or meet legal obligations.

Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information, and you may opt out of marketing at any time, by contacting us below.

Cookies

Our website may use cookies to improve your experience. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Effective date" above reflects the latest version.
